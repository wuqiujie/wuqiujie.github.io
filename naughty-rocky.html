PROJECT / NAUGHTY ROCKY

Naughty Rocky

A VR game on Oculus Quest 2 from the point of view of a naughty dog.

Programmer · Designer Oculus Quest 2 · VR Unity3D 2-week project · 2021/9/21–10/5
Original Wix Page ↗

Players become Rocky, a mischievous dog who must fix the mess before the owner returns.

Overview

Naughty Rocky is a VR game created for the Building Virtual Worlds course at CMU. Players see the world through Rocky’s eyes as he runs around the apartment, wreaks havoc and then scrambles to clean everything up when the owner announces she is on her way home.

Role & Responsibilities

I was responsible for core gameplay engineering, animation and brainstorming interactive moments.

  • Implemented object interactions such as opening drawers, spraying paint, pouring drinks and throwing trash.
  • Set up physics behaviours so actions feel playful but still readable to new VR users.
  • Helped design the overall flow from “chaos” to “clean-up” phase.

Theme & Design

The assignment combined the ideas of a naïve guest and a strong feeling of freedom. Our goal was to create a highly interactive environment where guests can almost do anything they can think of, without relying on long textual instructions. Visual cues, sound effects and Rocky’s perspective guide players instead.

Takeaways

This project taught me how to support first-time VR players: affordances need to be obvious, and feedback must be immediate so people feel confident experimenting with the environment.
