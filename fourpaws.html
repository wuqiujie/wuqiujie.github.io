PROJECT / FOUR PAWS ARE BETTER THAN ONE

Four Paws are Better Than One

A cooperative festival game using a DDR mat and MaKey MaKey.

Programmer · Designer DDR Mat + MaKey MaKey Unity3D 2-week project · 2021/11/2–11/16
Original Wix Page ↗ Download (GitHub) ↗

Two players cooperate using a dance mat and custom controller to solve mini-game challenges.

Overview

Four Paws are Better Than One is a physical-digital game built for the Entertainment Technology Center’s public game festival. Guests of all ages step on a DDR mat and use a MaKey MaKey controller to complete three mini-games together.

Hardware & Tools

  • Dance Dance Revolution Mat as the main movement input.
  • MaKey MaKey board wired into physical objects as additional buttons.
  • Unity3D as the engine for game logic and visuals.

Role & Contributions

  • Co-designed gameplay, puzzles and overall game-flow with the other programmer and two artists.
  • Implemented visual effects using particle systems, shaders and VFX graphs.
  • Built scenes, UI, animations and transitions between the three mini-games.

Level Design & Visuals

The three mini-games focus on cooperation: sorting papers, delivering boxes, and completing tasks under time pressure.

Festival Context

The theme for this round was “Festival”: visitors range from kids to industry professionals. That meant designing a game that feels welcoming, teaches itself quickly and remains fun even when a line of people is waiting to try it.
