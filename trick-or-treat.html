PROJECT / TRICK OR TREAT (AR)

Trick or Treat

A mixed-reality ghost-catching game on HoloLens 2.

Programmer · Designer HoloLens 2 · Mixed Reality Unity · C# 2-week project · 2021/9/7–9/21
Original Wix Page ↗

Halloween village scene where players catch ghosts and drag them into a magic cauldron.

Overview

Trick or Treat is a short AR experience where players wear a HoloLens 2 headset and rescue children from mischievous ghosts. The ghosts roam around a Halloween-themed environment, and players use hand tracking to grab and throw them into a glowing cauldron.

Theme & Core Idea

The assigned prompt was that the guest helps one character who is afraid of another. We turned this into a story about frightened children and friendly magic: the player acts as a protector who defeats ghosts instead of being scared by them.

Role & Implementation

  • Connected the Unity project to HoloLens 2 and debugged MR build issues.
  • Implemented game mechanics: ghost spawning, grabbing and throwing interactions.
  • Set up scenes, UI, animations and transitions between stages.
  • Created lighting, shaders and post-processing to enhance atmosphere.
  • Handled quality assurance and playtesting with classmates.

Pathfinding & Behaviour

To make ghosts feel alive, we used an A*-based pathfinding system to navigate them through the environment. Different ghost types move in distinct patterns, giving players a variety of challenges when timing their hand gestures.
