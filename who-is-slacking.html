PROJECT / WHO IS SLACKING (EYE TRACKER)

Who is Slacking

A small office game where your gaze reveals which employees are not working.

Programmer · Designer Tobii Eye Tracker 5 5-day project · 2021/10/6–10/11
Original Wix Page ↗ Download (GitHub) ↗

The player is the boss; gaze focuses on suspicious employees and triggers reactions.

Overview

In Who is Slacking, players become a manager watching over an open-plan office. Using an eye tracker, the game interprets the player’s gaze as a way to “inspect” employees. If the player stares at a lazy worker long enough, that character reacts in a humorous way.

Theme & Goal

The jam theme challenged us to build a fun, focus-driven experience in a week. I wanted to highlight how eye tracking can serve as both input and storytelling device—where simply looking at something can change the scene.

Role & Implementation

  • Implemented the full gameplay loop, from eye-gaze detection to employee reactions and camera movement.
  • Integrated Tobii Eye Tracker 5 and mapped gaze points to characters in the scene.
  • Co-designed game flow, pacing and difficulty with teammates.
