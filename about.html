About

Developer at the crossroads of computer graphics, HCI and game design — building playful, explorable virtual worlds.

Hi! I’m Qiujie (Kristin) Wu — a developer who loves mixing graphics, interaction and storytelling.

Hi! I’m Qiujie (Kristin) Wu, a developer who is fascinated by how computer graphics and human–computer interaction come together to shape experiences. I’ve built software, games and applications for many different platforms — including AR/VR, iOS, Android, web, eye tracking and CAVE systems. Each platform has taught me something new about how people see, touch and move through virtual worlds.

I’m an energetic and collaborative teammate. I’ve worked with other developers to create game systems, core technology and tools using Unity3D and Unreal Engine 4 with C#/C++. On multiple projects I’ve designed and implemented gameplay systems while keeping performance in mind across several target platforms.

My background also includes game design — from level design and game structure to gameplay engineering, UI design and project management. I enjoy rapid prototyping and playtesting in fast-paced environments where the quality bar is high and iteration is constant.

At the core, my passion is to build rich, explorable virtual worlds and interactive experiences. I love taking abstract ideas, translating them into systems and interactions, and then polishing them until they feel intuitive, expressive and fun for players and users.
